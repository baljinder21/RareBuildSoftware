Life Dashboard v1.0.0 — Your All-in-One Productivity Command Centre

📅 April 14, 2026 · 4 min read · By RBS Team

We're thrilled to launch Life Dashboard v1.0.0 — a free, all-in-one personal productivity app for Windows. One window. 16 powerful widgets. Everything you need to stay on top of your life, work, health, and finances.

16 Widgets in One App

Life Dashboard brings together tools that normally require multiple apps:

  • Tasks — to-do list with priorities and categories
  • Habits — daily habit tracker with streak counters
  • Finance — income/expense tracker with visual charts
  • Weather — live weather with 3-day forecast for any city
  • Calendar — monthly view with event management
  • Sleep — log sleep quality and view 7-day history
  • Diet Plan — meal planner with country-based templates (Indian, Mediterranean, Japanese, American, Korean, Mexican, Global)
  • Water — daily water intake tracker
  • Goals — long-term goal tracker with progress bars
  • Pomodoro — focus timer with 25/5/15 minute cycles
  • Notes — markdown editor with live preview
  • News — RSS feed reader (BBC, Hacker News, NYT)
  • Clock — world clocks for NYC, London, Tokyo, Sydney
  • Quick Links — bookmark launcher for favourite sites
  • Countdown — year/month/week/day progress and event countdowns
  • Quotes — daily motivational quotes

Fully Customisable Layout

Drag widgets by their header to rearrange them. Resize from the bottom-right corner. Show or hide any widget from the Widgets menu. Build the exact layout that works for your day.

Your Data Stays Private

All data is stored locally on your computer. Nothing is uploaded to any server. The only widgets that need internet are Weather (for forecasts) and News (for RSS feeds). Everything else works completely offline.

System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • 100 MB free disk space
  • 4 GB RAM minimum
  • No administrator rights required
